Project Overview

River Island Gym includes a fitness center, swimming pool and sauna, indoor skate park, three-story climbing gym, immersive adventure playground, full-size basketball court, multiple mezzanines with slides and arcade games, community gathering spaces, and a beverage bar and lounge area.

The layout of the property invites guests to enjoy multiple activities ranging from jungle gym play to challenging rock climbing routes to aquatic games, interspersed with stops for food, drink, rest, water bottle refill, showers, and bathrooms.

While each feature and program has its own space, they intentionally overlap and blend, creating a hive of energy and connection. At its heart, RIG is a cultural crossroads: climbers, skaters, swimmers, and lifters will feel at home, and so will seniors, curious kids, and anyone simply seeking connection. Whether you’re active or just along for the ride, RIG invites everyone to gather, play, and belong.

Mission

River Island Gym stands as a joyful expression of community-building. Part fitness center, part playground, part gathering place, and part act of defiance, RIG cultivates movement, curiosity, a sense of shared belonging, and the belief that enjoying life together matters.

Vision

River Island Gym redefines what recreation can mean for rural communities through innovative design and programming. By creating a place where people of all ages and backgrounds can thrive, RIG serves as a pilot solution that addresses health, isolation, and opportunity gaps in Madison County.

Purpose

For rural Madison County, River Island Gym provides a place where residents can enjoy life together - an unconventional community hub that blends playground- style exploration, whole body exercise, and social opportunity for all ages.
